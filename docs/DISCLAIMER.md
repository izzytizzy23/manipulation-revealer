## Not a Diagnostic Tool

Persuasion Shield's analysis, influence scores, and neural mechanism references are:

- Based on rule-based pattern matching, not AI classification
- Approximations, not precise measurements
- Informed by published neuroscience research, not direct brain measurement
- Subject to false positives (flagging non-manipulative content) and false negatives (missing manipulative content)

The extension does not claim to:

- Determine the intent of content creators
- Diagnose psychological manipulation with clinical accuracy
- Measure actual neural responses or brain activity
- Provide a legally admissible assessment of deceptive practices
