## 3. Accuracy and Limitations

### 3.1 No Guarantee of Accuracy

The Extension uses rule-based pattern matching and may produce:

- **False positives:** Flagging content that is not actually manipulative
- **False negatives:** Missing content that is genuinely manipulative
- **Context errors:** Misidentifying the context or intent of content

The influence scores, risk levels, and tactic classifications are **approximations** based on pattern matching and publicly available research. They are not precise measurements of persuasive intent or neural impact.

### 3.2 TRIBE v2 Methodology Disclaimer

The Extension's weighting system is **informed by** publicly available research on Meta's TRIBE v2 model. The Extension:

- Does **not** use, integrate with, or access TRIBE v2 directly
- Does **not** measure actual neural activity or brain responses
- Does **not** claim clinical, scientific, or diagnostic accuracy
- Uses published research as a framework for weighting, not as a validated instrument

### 3.3 Regulatory Reference Disclaimer

References to specific regulations (EU AI Act, GDPR, FTC Act, PIPEDA, CPPA, Australian Privacy Act, etc.) are:

- Provided for **educational and informational purposes only**
